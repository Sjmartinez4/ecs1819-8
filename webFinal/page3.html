Movies

Superhero Movies

Avengers:Infinity War

This had been my most anticipated movie of 2018 and it did not disapoint. It is probably my favorite movie, but Endgame might change that. I still get chills when watching certain scenes. This movie is too good.

The Dark Knight

Even though I am a Marvel Nerd, my favorite hero is Batman. Anything that has to do with Batman is good for me. I just love the character so much due to all his gadgets and technology. Pretty much the same reason I love Iron-Man. This movie is just awesome though. Heath Ledger does an amazing job as the Joker.

Aquaman

Aquaman was looking like a good movie based off the trailers and it did not disapoint. The CGI was actually really good and I have to say, Black Manta was clearly the best character despite him not being the main villain. He has always been one of my favorite villains and his design for the movie was so good. The action scenes were really good which made this movie very enjoyable.

Other Movies

Jurassic World

I have always loved Dinosaurs, and the Jurassic park movies have always been top movies for me. Specifically, Jurassic World is my favorite of the 5 that have released.

War for the Planet of the Apes

This movie is so good. Andy Serkis does a phenomenal job as Caesar and this movie had so many emotional moments. The story was so well done and the characters were very well portrayed.

Godzilla

Godzilla is such a beast. I just love to see huge monsters fight each other and I can't wait to watch him fight Rodan, King Ghidorah, and Mothra in the new movie.
